An Explanation on Me

My Why and How I am Going to Get There

The main goal I have is to learn as much as possible. The more education I have the more I know and the better I can understand the world. To formulate my own identity I need to know what is out there. I can’t make wise decisions if I do not see more than my tiny section of the picture. It may be impossible for anybody to see the full picture. However, I want to be able to see as much as possible so I need to do my best to reach the impossible.

Through the classes I have taken, I have learned about various topics and reached a higher level of understanding. Through the people I have met, I have learned from different perspectives and gained a better understanding of those around me. Through living on my own I have learned and will continue to learn to be a capable and independent adult. Every aspect of life is an opportunity to learn something. The environment of BYU cultivates both new growth and learning and reinforces things already learned.

As of now, I am planning on applying to the Experience Design Management program. I do not know exactly how my future will plan out or if I will be accepted into that program but I know that learning what I can while at this age will help propel me throughout the rest of my life. I don’t know what I want to do professionally or if I even want a profession, but I do know that education can have a greater impact than simply getting a job.

I can’t expect perfection in my professional and educational endeavors but I know I will try. I have a strong belief in doing my best. If I am not doing my best I am wasting the opportunity to improve.

Along with doing my best is the idea of doing hard things. I can’t learn and grow if I am not challenging myself and pushing myself to my limits. College isn’t easy but I embrace the challenge and opportunity to better myself intellectually. Not only is it beneficial for my current self but all of the growth and learning I do now can benefit me for the rest of my life.
